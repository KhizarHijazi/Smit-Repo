Quaid-e-Azam Residency

Escape to the serene mountains of Ziarat and experience the historic Quaid-e-Azam Residency. Immerse yourself in the aura of this magnificent colonial-era building where Pakistan's founding father, Quaid-e-Azam Muhammad Ali Jinnah, spent his last days. Wander through the beautifully preserved rooms, surrounded by lush gardens and breathtaking mountain views. Discover the legacy of a great leader and pay homage to his remarkable contributions. Quaid-e-Azam Residency in Ziarat, a must-visit destination for history enthusiasts and those seeking solace in nature's embrace..
